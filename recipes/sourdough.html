Sourdough dough

Ingredients

  • Sourdough starter
  • Spelt or Rye flour ( or mix)
  • salt
  • Water
  • Patience and Love

Steps

  1. 100g starter , add to water and whisk it
  2. 310g water , 25c
  3. 450g spelt flour, add to a water and make your hands busy
  4. Add 8g salt , and mix until compact

Sourdough pancakes

Ingredients

  • sourdough starter
  • egg
  • water
  • coconut oil
  • protein vanilla powder
  • brown sugar
  • salt

Steps

  1. Preheat a griddle to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C). Lightly grease the griddle.
  2. Combine sourdough starter, egg, water, and oil in a large bowl; mix well.
  3. Combine protein powder, sugar, and salt in a separate bowl. Stir protein powder mixture into starter mixture until batter is smooth.
  4. Pour batter onto the preheated griddle to form 8 pancakes and cook until the bottoms are golden brown. Flip and continue cooking until the opposite sides are browned.
